About Us

I started Sweet Cleaning in 2011 because I know how important it is to spend time with family. I was working in the food service industry at the time - arriving to work in the morning when it was still dark outside and not making it home until dinner. I knew that I was missing precious time with my two sons that I could never get back.

That's how Sweet Cleaning was born! I'm passionate about what I do because not only did it give me back quality time with my own family, it lets me help others find more time for the people and things they love. As the business grew, I knew I would need people on my team who shared my vision of delivering top-notch service that is affordable and reliable. That's why I brought on my sister, Gaby. We are family owned and operated, so you will always know the crew who is coming to your house.

We are so happy that we get to work together doing something we care about - putting smiles on our clients' faces! There is nothing like coming home to a fresh, clean house to make your day, and we love to help our clients find more time to relax and unwind. Give us a call today and let my family help you get more time with yours!

Residential Service Specialists

Your home, cleaned your way. We specialize in residential service and would love to knock a spotless house off your to-do list. Leave the spring cleaning to us, or set up recurring service and reclaim your free time! Every home is different, so please contact us for a quote so we can discuss your space and your preferences. Services you can expect in a standard cleaning include:

  • Vacuum/mop floors
  • Dust/wipe surfaces & disinfect as needed
  • Empty trash cans
  • Scrub showers and tubs
  • Dust blinds
  • Make beds
  • Disinfect bathroom surfaces
  • Clean mirrors

Service Upgrades

  • Wash & fold laundry
  • Clean fridge interior
  • Clean oven
  • Wash & change bed linens

Need a job that you don't see listed? Drop us a line and describe what you need. We try our best to be accommodating!
